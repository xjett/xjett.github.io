Who We Are

About Us

London PDP, is London's local Damp & Preservation Expert.

We take pride in our memberships with The Property Care Association, SafeContractor, and Trustmark. Our clientele includes homeowners, property management/estate agents, landlords, property developers, and other property professionals. Each client has unique needs, and we tailor our approach accordingly. Regardless of project size, we assist with damp, timber issues, and basement conversions or refurbishments. Our client relationships rely on recommendations and word of mouth, emphasizing our dedication to customer service, reliability, and quality.

We exclusively use certified products and adhere to British Standard practices. Endorsed by the SafeContractor scheme, our work maintains the highest standards. With extensive experience, we offer guidance on damp, moisture problems, plastering, and rendering. Our Certified Surveyors of Remedial Treatments provide detailed reports, known for their honesty and integrity. We prioritize minimal disruption during our work, recognizing its potential for noise and dust. We're available to offer advice and provide quotes without obligation. Our treatments are guaranteed, and we offer insured guarantees through Specialist Building Guarantees for added peace of mind.
